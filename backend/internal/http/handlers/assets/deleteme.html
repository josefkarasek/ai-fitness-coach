Account Deletion

Delete your account

You can delete your account directly in the The Forge app, or request deletion by email if you no longer have access to the app.

Delete in the app

You can delete your account directly in the The Forge app:

Settings → Account → Delete account

Delete by email

If you no longer have access to the app, request deletion by emailing: support@liftsforge.com

Use the subject “Delete my The Forge account” and send the request from the email address associated with your account.

Deleting your account permanently removes your login account and associated application data. Some records may be retained where legally required, as described in our privacy policy.
